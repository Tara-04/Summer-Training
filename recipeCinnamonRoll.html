The speciality of our shop is CINNAMON ROLL

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of butter

Steps

  1. Take a bowl and add flour, sugar, milk and butter
  2. Now mix all the ingredients well
  3. Now take a pan and add the mixture in it
  4. Now cook it for 10 minutes
  5. Now take a plate and add the mixture in it
  6. Now add cinnamon powder and sugar
  7. Now roll it
  8. Now cut it into pieces
  9. Now serve it
